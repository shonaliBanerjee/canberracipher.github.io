Canberra CIPHER

We are Canberra CIPHER, the only FIRST Robotics team based in the ACT. We inspire an interest in robotics in young people through student led teaching with the support of experienced mentors - no previous experience required! If you are interested in electronics, CAD, animation, photography or engineering, why not come along to a session and check it out!
